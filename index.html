Hot air balloon tours

in beautiful locations

Sky Journeys was established in 2021 with the aim to provide amazing hot air balloon tours. We currently operate in 5 different countries and look forward to expanding further.

Let us show you how holidays should be!

Magnificent Locations

Each journey explores stunning areas and landmarks from a unique perspective in a hot air balloon. We take you on routes covering approximately 25 miles per day. From Norwegian Fjords to the Grand Canyon in Arizona, USA, there is a location to suit anyone.

Expert Guides

Our team of guides have been specially selected and regularly tested - some include world renowned masters of hot air balloons. Every tour includes a personal guide for up to 5 people. Safety is paramount and, before the trip begins, there is a brief training course to prepare you.

Sight-Seeing

There is plenty of opportunity for viewing the scenery in an unrestrictive view and no queues. See iconic landmarks like you never have before!

Food & Drink Included

Meals and beverages are part of the package. Depending on the location, this may be prepared by our guides or at a restaurant. After booking, we will contact you for any dietary requirements.

Groups up to 20 people

We are able to arrange special group only journeys for 10-20 people. A representative from our team will contact you, if the booking is made for at least 10 people, to offer this option. Additional activities and specific requests can be made to personalise the experience.

Camping or hotels provided

For tours that cover more than 1 day, tents are available at no extra cost. Depending on the location, we are able to arrange hotel rooms at a low additional charge - this will include transportation to and from the hotel.
